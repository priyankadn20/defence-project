About Rongchondo

Rongchondo is an innovative platform designed to empower visually impaired users. Our system transforms images into clear Bangla captions, enabling everyone to access visual content effortlessly.

Using advanced AI and image processing, we ensure that each image can be read aloud by screen readers. This creates an inclusive digital experience, bridging the accessibility gap for the visually impaired community.
